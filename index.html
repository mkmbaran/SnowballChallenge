"Globally, at least 2 billion people use a drinking

Just because drinking water appears clear, does not guarantee it isn't contaminated.
Our product helps detect bacteria and pathogens even in crystal clear water sources.
We value health and safety, and want people around the globe to know if what they are drinking is safe or not
by developing a new chemical concoction to register the purity of water we have been made it possible
to discover whether or not your drinking water really is safe to drink.

The product works by inserting one pill into the water and waiting for it to quickly dissolve.
The water will then change color depending on how dangerous the pathogens in the water are.

DISCLAIMER: These pills are not for direct consumption, please apply them into your drink.

Blue is safe to drink.

Yellow is potentially contaminated.

Red is hazardous to consume.
